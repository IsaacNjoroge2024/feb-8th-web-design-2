Australia

Australia, officially the Commonwealth of Australia, is a sovereign country comprising the mainland of the Australian continent, the island of Tasmania, and numerous smaller islands. It is the largest country in Oceania and the world's sixth-largest country by total area. The term "Australia" is also often used to refer to the continent of Australia, which includes the mainland, Tasmania, New Guinea, and other nearby islands.

Some Important Countries in Oceania:

  • Australia - The largest country in Oceania
  • New Zealand - Known for its stunning landscapes and Maori culture
  • Papua New Guinea - One of the world's most culturally diverse countries
  • Fiji - An island nation known for its tropical beauty
  • Solomon Islands - An archipelago nation in the South Pacific
