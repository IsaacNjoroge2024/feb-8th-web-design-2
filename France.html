France

France, officially the French Republic, is a country primarily located in Western Europe. It also includes overseas regions and territories in the Americas and the Atlantic, Pacific and Indian Oceans, giving it one of the largest discontiguous exclusive economic zones in the world. Its metropolitan area extends from the Rhine to the Atlantic Ocean and from the Mediterranean Sea to the English Channel and the North Sea.

Key Facts about France:

  • Capital: Paris
  • Largest City: Paris
  • Official Language: French
  • Population: About 67 million
  • Known for: Art, fashion, cuisine, wine, the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and historical significance
