Asia

Asia is Earth's largest and most populous continent, located primarily in the Eastern and Northern Hemispheres. It shares the continental landmass of Eurasia with the continent of Europe and the continental landmass of Afro-Eurasia with both Europe and Africa. Asia covers an area of about 44.6 million square kilometers (17.2 million square miles), about 30% of Earth's total land area and 8.7% of the Earth's total surface area.

Some Important Countries in Asia:

  • China - The world's most populous country with a rich history
  • India - The second most populous country known for its diverse culture
  • Japan - An island nation known for its technology and traditions
  • Saudi Arabia - Home to Islam's holiest sites
  • Russia - The world's largest country, spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia
