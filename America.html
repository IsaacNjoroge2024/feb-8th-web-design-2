America

The Americas, also known as America, consist of two continents: North America and South America. They occupy the western hemisphere of Earth and are surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean to the east and the Pacific Ocean to the west.

Some Important Countries in America:

  • United States - A global superpower with diverse geography and culture
  • Canada - The second largest country in the world by total area
  • Brazil - The largest country in South America
  • Mexico - Rich in history with ancient Mayan and Aztec ruins
  • Argentina - Known for its diverse landscapes from the Andes to the Pampas
